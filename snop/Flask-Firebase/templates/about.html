About Snop

Snop is a simple, modern and engaging Norwegian learning app designed for students, newcomers, and anyone wanting to improve their Norwegian skills.

The app includes challenges, progress tracking, pronunciation tools, and mini-tasks to help you build your vocabulary day by day.

Our mission is to make learning Norwegian fun, effective and consistent — one “snop” at a time.
