# Norwegian Language Learning App - Quick Start Guide

## What is this app?

A mobile app designed to help you learn Norwegian by encouraging real-world conversations with strangers through fun challenges!

## How It Works

### 1. Daily Challenges (10-15 points)
Quick, easy tasks you can do every day:
- Say "God morgen" (Good morning) to someone
- Thank someone in Norwegian
- Order coffee at a cafÃ©

### 2. Weekly Challenges (50-75 points)
More involved challenges that push your comfort zone:
- Have a 5-minute conversation
- Ask about the weather with 3 different people
- Complete a shopping trip speaking only Norwegian

### 3. Monthly Challenges (200-250 points)
Big goals that require dedication:
- Make a Norwegian friend
- Join a local event and talk to 5 people
- Make a phone call in Norwegian

## Tips for Success

1. **Start Small**: Begin with daily challenges to build confidence
2. **Be Brave**: The best learning happens outside your comfort zone
3. **Practice Daily**: Try to complete at least one challenge every day
4. **Don't Worry About Mistakes**: Norwegians appreciate the effort!
5. **Have Fun**: Make it a game - challenge yourself to earn more points

## Norwegian Phrases to Get Started

- **God morgen** - Good morning
- **Tusen takk** - Thank you very much
- **Hvor er...?** - Where is...?
- **Kan jeg fÃ¥ en kaffe?** - Can I have a coffee?
- **Jeg heter...** - My name is...
- **Hva heter du?** - What is your name?

## Scoring System

- Daily challenges: 10-15 points
- Weekly challenges: 50-75 points
- Monthly challenges: 200-250 points

Track your progress and try to beat your own high score!

## Need Help?

If you're new to speaking Norwegian:
1. Practice the phrases at home first
2. Start with simple greetings
3. Use gestures to help communicate
4. Smile - it's a universal language!
